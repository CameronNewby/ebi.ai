Overview

If you’re looking for an indispensable new team member, you just found it. That team member is a chatbot…

A programmable virtual agent that automatically responds in natural language to limitless user requests as soon as they come in.

Consumer enquiries… FAQs… transactions… pretty much anything your call centre would normally deal with. You name it, a chatbot handles it like a pro.

The industry use possibilities are endless. Chatbots interact with consumers, are trained to make decisions without asking a superior, and take the weight off your call centre (and shoulders) as a result.

You don’t have to add them to payroll or continually pay to train them, but they always turn up and you’re always learning. Whether you’re looking to automate claims processing, offer technical support, or take hotel reservations, a chatbot does it all with ease and exceptional reliability. There’s no chance of it going rogue, iRobot style.
